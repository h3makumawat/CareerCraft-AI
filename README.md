# CareerCraft AI - Resume & LinkedIn Coach

## Overview
CareerCraft AI is a Generative AI-powered platform designed to help students and job seekers improve their resumes, optimize LinkedIn profiles, and prepare for career opportunities.

## Problem Statement
Many students struggle to create professional resumes and present their skills effectively on LinkedIn.

## Proposed Solution
An AI-based assistant that provides resume analysis, ATS improvement suggestions, LinkedIn optimization tips, and interview preparation support.

## Planned Features
- AI Resume Analyzer
- ATS Resume Score
- LinkedIn Profile Improvement Suggestions
- Professional Summary Generator
- Interview Question Generator
